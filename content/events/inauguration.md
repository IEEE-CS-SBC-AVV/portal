## About This Event

Join us for the official inauguration of IEEE Computer Society Student Branch Chapter at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham!

**Date:** February 26, 2026  
**Time:** 3:00 PM - 4:30 PM  
**Location:** LH-220  
**Speaker:** Dr. Arun, IEEE CS Secretary of Madras Region

### Event Highlights

This inaugural ceremony will feature:

- **Chapter Logo Reveal** - Introduction of the official IEEE CS SBC AVV branding and identity
- **Vision & Mission Overview** - Understanding our chapter's goals and objectives for the academic year
- **4-Month Action Plan** - Strategic initiatives and events planned for the upcoming months
- **Leadership Introduction** - Meet the executive committee and core team members
- **Networking Opportunity** - Connect with fellow IEEE CS members and enthusiasts

### What to Expect

This is an exciting opportunity to be part of our chapter's journey as we embark on initiatives focused on:

- Technical skill development through workshops and seminars
- Industry connections and networking events
- Collaborative projects and hackathons
- Professional growth and career opportunities

All IEEE members and interested students are welcome to attend!

### About the Speaker

**Dr. Arun** brings extensive experience from his role as IEEE Computer Society Secretary in the Madras Region. He will share insights on IEEE's global mission and the opportunities available through our student chapter.
